# Community Guidelines & Moderation

Created: 2024-01-25

## Overview

This document outlines VTubersTV's community guidelines and moderation policies, designed to create a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable environment for all users.

## Core Principles

1. **Transparency**
   - All moderation actions are logged publicly
   - Clear reasoning for decisions
   - Open appeals process
   - Regular community updates

2. **Fairness**
   - Consistent rule enforcement
   - Cultural sensitivity
   - Context consideration
   - Proportional responses

3. **Education First**
   - Focus on teaching over punishment
   - Clear explanations of violations
   - Resources for improvement
   - Community mentorship

## Community Guidelines

### Content Standards

1. **Age Verification & Content Access**
   - Platform accessible to users 13+
   - NSFW content requires:
     - Age verification (18+)
     - Government ID verification
     - Clear NSFW content tagging
   - Family-friendly hours maintained:
     - JST: 12:00 PM - 8:00 PM
     - PT: 7:00 PM - 3:00 AM (previous day)
     - UTC: 3:00 AM - 11:00 AM
     - CET: 4:00 AM - 12:00 PM
     - AEST: 1:00 PM - 9:00 PM
     - IST: 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM
     - BRT: 12:00 AM - 8:00 AM
     - EST: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
     - CST: 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM
     - MST: 6:00 AM - 2:00 PM
     - HST: 5:00 AM - 1:00 PM

2. **Language & Behavior**
   - No hate speech or discrimination
   - No harassment or bullying
   - Respectful disagreement encouraged
   - Multi-language support

3. **Stream Content**
   - No copyright infringement
   - Mandatory content warnings
   - Appropriate thumbnails
   - Accurate stream titles and tags
   - NSFW streams must be properly marked

### Prohibited Content

1. **Zero Tolerance**
   - Illegal activities
   - Hate speech
   - Doxxing
   - Harassment campaigns

2. **Age-Restricted Content**
   - Explicit adult content (18+ verified only)
   - Gore or violence
   - Excessive profanity
   - Controversial topics
   - Political content

2. **Channel Moderators**

3. **AI Assistance**

## Enforcement

### Warning System

1. **First Offense**
   - Warning message
   - Educational resources
   - 24-hour chat restriction
   - Required guideline review

2. **Second Offense**
   - 7-day restriction
   - Required training
   - Moderator review
   - Appeal option

3. **Third Offense**
   - 30-day restriction
   - Comprehensive review
   - Appeal board review
   - Rehabilitation plan

### Ban Types

1. **Temporary Bans**
   - Duration: 1-30 days
   - Clear explanation
   - Appeal available
   - Return conditions

2. **Permanent Bans**
   - Severe violations
   - Multiple offenses
   - Appeal board review
   - Possible rehabilitation

## Appeals Process

### Filing Appeals

1. **Requirements**
   - Written explanation
   - Acknowledgment of violation
   - Improvement plan
   - Community reference

2. **Process**
   - Submit appeal form
   - Moderator review
   - Community input
   - Final decision

### Review Board

1. **Composition**
   - Community moderators
   - Platform staff
   - Community representatives
   - Content creators

2. **Decision Making**
   - Evidence review
   - Context consideration
   - Precedent check
   - Majority vote
